Greek Language Courses

From complete beginner to advanced fluency, Ioanna's comprehensive courses combine language learning with cultural immersion for a truly authentic Greek education experience.

Course Levels

Ioanna offers structured Greek courses across five proficiency levels. Select the level that matches your current skills to learn more about what you'll master.

A1

Beginner

Learn the Greek alphabet, master basic pronunciation, and practice essential phrases for everyday interactions and greetings.

A2

Elementary

Build on your foundation with everyday vocabulary and grammar. Engage in simple conversations about shopping, dining, and daily routines.

B1

Intermediate

Strengthen your grammar and expand your vocabulary. Practice conversation skills for travel, daily life, and cultural topics with confidence.

B2

Upper Intermediate

Improve fluency and comprehension to handle complex discussions. Gain the skills to express opinions and debate a variety of topics with ease.

C1

Advanced

Achieve near-native proficiency by mastering advanced vocabulary, idiomatic expressions, and the cultural nuances of everyday Greek life.
