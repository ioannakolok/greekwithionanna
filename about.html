Ioanna's Teaching Philosophy

Ioanna believes that language learning should be a joyful journey of cultural discovery, not just a series of grammar rules and vocabulary lists.

Learn Naturally – the Brain’s Way

I guide students to feel the language through patterns, repetition, and examples in real context. Step by step, the brain builds automaticity (as neuroscience confirms) through exposure, practice, and use.

Active Learning through Doing

I speak little, ask much. Using the Socratic (μαιευτική) approach, I help students form answers, find rules through examples, and speak from day one. Learning happens through doing, guessing, and correcting.

Personalized Learning

Each student learns differently, so I adapt lessons to their pace, goals, and style. I observe what helps them learn best and adjust accordingly — making progress feel natural, personal, and enjoyable.

Motivation, Warmth, and Joy

Languages need effort. And we make that effort enjoyable together! With humor, encouragement, and empathy, I create a space where students feel safe to try, make mistakes, and grow. Progress is celebrated, curiosity is nurtured, and confidence blooms.
